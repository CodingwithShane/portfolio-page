About me!

Well I'm just your typical everyday aussie that wants to code and create websites all day and everyday. I done it so much that I'm starting to get fairly decent with it.

In this digital age where almost everything has gone digital, there are some businesses still behind the times. For example I wanted to get a pizza and there was one pizza shop that was recommended to me so I tried looking for a site. There was nothing. Went to google and found a number but no site. So I called up and after some time someone answered and I asked what type of pizzas you have? Obviously the lady on the phone was clearly annoyed as she had a full shop and I was asking for the menu. Now what is the morale of the story? Go digital so more customers can find you and give you service. No more hogging the phone lines or annoying people asking what you got.

What skills do I have? I am currently profcient in HTML, CSS and Javascript. If you don't know what they are then it just means I can make pretty web sites that does some cool things if you so wish.

When I create a site for you. I put my heart and soul into it. It's as if your business is my business (which it is as you would be hiring me) and I will only make what I would make for myself.
